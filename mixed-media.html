Persona 5 Lightbox

2023 / Chipboard and Arduino UNO circuitry

WEST 107: Maker Workshop

  This lightbox is themed after the 2016 JRPG Persona 5, featuring each of the main Phantom Thieves, the PT logo, and the Metaverse Navigator icon on the cutout sides. A bundle of red LEDs are wired to a coin battery on the inside of the box, which rests atop another box that houses the wiring for the music player, which plays a short version of P5's battle theme, Last Surprise (using this as a reference). This is also shown on the LCD, for which a picture is included above. The box and all its cutouts are laser cut and engraved.

  As a side note, the LCD was meant to display scrolling messages, including the “Now Playing” text. Unfortunately, the music player and the scrolling LCD could not be run in the same loop—these needed their own loops (and thus, their own cores/Arduinos) in order to function properly.
